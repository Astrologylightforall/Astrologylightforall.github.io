Our Story

I come from a family where tradition, trust, and helping others has always been a way of life. While our family has been in the jewellery business for many years, our true connection has always been with astrology. My father has been a full-time astrologer for over 30 years, and I've seen him guide people with care, honesty, and deep knowledge.

My journey into astrology began when I was just 13. I would sit beside my father as he read Kundali and shared insights with people. I didn't fully understand everything back then, but I was drawn to it. Slowly, I started learning more about Kundali analysis and numerology—and before I knew it, it became a part of who I am.

My elder brother, who is 16, is also learning this ancient science alongside me. Together, we are building our knowledge and carrying forward our family's tradition of guidance and care.

Now, with over 17 years of experience, I work alongside my father, and together we help people find clarity in different areas of life—whether it's career, love, marriage, business, money matters, or property issues.

At Astrology Light For All, we don't just read charts—we listen, we guide, and we care. Every person we meet has a unique story, and we feel blessed to be able to help them find light and direction on their journey.
